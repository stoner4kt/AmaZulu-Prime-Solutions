FAQ

Common Questions from Homeowners & Businesses

If you need more details, call us on 061 314 0218 or request a free quote.

1) How quickly can I get a quote from AmaZulu Prime Solutions?

We usually respond within 24 hours. For urgent jobs we can provide same-day call-backs and prioritised site assessments in Durban and surrounding areas.

2) Which areas do you serve?

We serve Durban and wider KwaZulu-Natal, including Umbumbulu, Umlazi, Pinetown, and nearby towns. Contact us if your location is outside these areas and we will confirm availability.

3) Do you offer emergency plumbing or electrical support?

Yes. We assist with urgent breakdowns such as leaks, blocked systems, power issues, and critical faults. Share photos/videos on WhatsApp for faster triage.

4) Are your technicians qualified and compliant?

Yes. Our team works to South African standards and best practices. We focus on safety, quality workmanship, and regulatory compliance on every project.

5) What HVAC services do you provide?

We handle HVAC installation, preventive maintenance, repairs, and performance optimisation for homes, offices, and commercial facilities.

6) Can you manage full renovations and not just one trade?

Absolutely. We coordinate multi-trade projects including plumbing, electrical, HVAC, painting, and finishes, helping you work with one accountable contractor.

7) How do you price projects?

Pricing is based on site conditions, project scope, materials, and labour requirements. We provide transparent, itemised quotations with no hidden costs.

8) Do you work with both residential and commercial clients?

Yes. We service private homes, sectional title properties, retail spaces, offices, and other commercial facilities across KwaZulu-Natal.

9) How do I book a job?

Use the quote form on the homepage, call us directly, or message us on WhatsApp. We will arrange a site visit and confirm your timeline.

10) What makes AmaZulu Prime Solutions different?

Clients choose us for reliable communication, high workmanship standards, cost-effective delivery, and dependable after-service support.
